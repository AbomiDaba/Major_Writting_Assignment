My choice of form impacted my goal in a very positive way. I believe that in order to show that art can be used in way to protest and make societal changes, the best way would be to share those images with explanations for context. So, with that being said, having an exhibition seemed to be the best way to get that done. This form allowed me to have a plethora of art pieces to show and make my connections in relation to my argument. The down side to an exhibition is that my explanations are limited when at times more said may be necessary.

I actually had a lot of changes from the beginning stages of this major writing assignment to this final piece. Originally, I planned to focus either on the Harlem Renaissance or police brutality. As I was researching my inquiring questions I realized there wasn’t much information that I could glean from, at least to where I would be satisfied. I continued my research and came across a reoccurring theme which is art addressing systemic injustices. That topic caught my interest as I continued to research, and I decided to go with that theme which worked out well for me.

Unfortunately we don’t hear the impact that art makes in so many sectors of life. We think that art is just for entertainment purposes, which part of that is true but I realized how there have been so many impactful and important artists that makes their purpose in their art to make a statement that will improve society. This really gave me a sense of confidence in my argument because I already knew this method that is employed by artists is effective, but I never realized how much it truly impacted different parts of society, even in different parts of the world.

I am most satisfied with the range of inequalities I addressed. I tried to make sure I presented different kinds of issues that different minorities face, so having versatility in issues gave me a sense of satisfaction. This works well because, first off, systemic inequality has diversity. So, addressing the different kinds of injustices and seeing them addressed through art makes this major writing assignment more appealing to my audience I believe. The only thing I would really apply would be the research aspect of this class because the reality is in almost everything you do professionally requires some kind of research.

As far as the content of my major writing assignment, I don’t think I would change much of what I have besides maybe adding a couple more images of art that address more kinds of systemic injustices.
